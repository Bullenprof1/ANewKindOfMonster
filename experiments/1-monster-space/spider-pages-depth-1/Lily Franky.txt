Born Masaya Nakagawa (1963-11-04) 4 November 1963 (age 57) Fukuoka, Japan Other names Elvis Woodstock Occupation Actor, writer, illustrator, designer, musician, lyricist, photographer, essayist Years active 1980s–present Height 174 cm (5 ft 9 in) Lily Franky (リリー・フランキー, Rirī Furankī, born Masaya Nakagawa (中川 雅也, Nakagawa Masaya); 4 November 1963) is a Japanese illustrator, writer and actor.[1] He has appeared in more than forty films since 2001.
