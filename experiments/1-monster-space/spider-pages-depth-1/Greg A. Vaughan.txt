Greg A. Vaughan is an American writer who creates material for roleplaying games. Vaughan's role-playing credits for the Dungeons & Dragons role-playing game include Drow of the Underdark,[1] Scepter Tower of Spellgard,[2] Anauroch: The Empire of Shade,[3] and The Twilight Tomb.[4] He was written several adventures for Dungeon magazine and Paizo's Pathfinder and GameMastery lines.
