It is a sequel to the 2004 television film, Raptor Island. Steven Bauer returned in this sequel playing a different role. It was entirely filmed in Romania. The film premiered in the United States on 25 January 2009 but was previously released on DVD in Brazil and Japan. Contents 1 Alternate titles 2 Plot 3 Cast 4 External links Alternate titles[edit] The film is also known as Raptor Planet 2, Raptor Island 2: Raptor Planet, Jurassic Planet. Plot[edit] In 2066, the planet is entirely occupied by raptors. The only hope for the human race is a group of marines.
