Rodney Thompson Born (1980-03-08) March 8, 1980 (age 41) Chattanooga, Tennessee Occupation Author Nationality American Subject Role-playing games Notable works Star Wars Roleplaying Game (Wizards of the Coast) Rodney Thompson is a game designer whose writing credits include numerous books for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game and the Dungeons & Dragons fantasy role-playing game.
