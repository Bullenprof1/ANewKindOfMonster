Dark Star Soundtrack album by John Carpenter Released 1980 Recorded 1973 Genre Electronic film score Length 51:01 Label Citadel (original release) We Release Whatever The Fuck We Want (2016 release) John Carpenter chronology Halloween (1979) Dark Star (1980) Escape from New York (1981) Dark Star is a soundtrack by John Carpenter for the 1974 film of the same name. While recorded in 1973, it wasn't released by Citadel until 1980, after the 1979 re-release of the film and the success of Carpenter's 1978 film Halloween. A limited expanded edition was released in 2016 through We Release Whatever The Fuck We Want Records.[1] Track listing[edit] No. Title Length 1.
