Display devices or content having horizontal resolution of approximately 2,000 pixels List of common display resolutions 2K resolution is a generic term for display devices or content having horizontal resolution of approximately 2,000 pixels.[1] In the movie projection industry, Digital Cinema Initiatives is the dominant standard for 2K output and defines 2K resolution as 2048 × 1080.[2][3] In television and consumer media, 1920 × 1080 is the most common 2K resolution, but this is normally referred to as 1080p.
