It was owned by All Times Publishing LLC. The publication was released every Wednesday, printing 36,000 copies and distributed to approximately 1150 locations in Central New York.[1] After an unsuccessful trial as a paid subscription-based paper, the New Times published its final issue on June 26, 2019.[2] References[edit] ^ "Syracuse New Times".
