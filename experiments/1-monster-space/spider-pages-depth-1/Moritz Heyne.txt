Moritz Heyne Moritz Heyne (June 8, 1837 in Weißenfels – March 1, 1906 in Göttingen) was a German Germanic linguist (Germanist). He taught as a professor at the University of Halle (1869-1870), University of Basel (1870-), University of Göttingen (1883-). He worked with Jakob Grimm to edit his dictionary after 1867.
