Sansei Shiomi 塩見 三省 Born (1948-01-12) January 12, 1948 (age 73) Kyoto Prefecture, Japan Occupation Actor Years active 1978–present Height 1.77 m (5 ft 9 1⁄2 in) Sansei Shiomi (塩見 三省, Shiomi Sansei, born 12 January 1948) is a Japanese actor. Contents 1 Career 2 Selected filmography 2.1 Film 2.2 Television 3 References 4 External links Career[edit] Born in Kyoto Prefecture, Shiomi went to Doshisha University.[1] He joined the theater troupe En in 1978[1][2] and soon also began appearing in film and television, mostly as a character actor.
