Lost Themes II Studio album by John Carpenter Released April 15, 2016 (2016-04-15) Genre Electronic Length 47:22 (CD/digital) 42:52 (vinyl) Label Sacred Bones John Carpenter chronology Lost Themes Remixed (2015) Lost Themes II (2016) Classic Themes Redux EP (2016) Lost Themes II is the second studio album by American film director and composer John Carpenter. It was released on April 15, 2016, through Sacred Bones Records.[1] The album was created in collaboration with Carpenter's son Cody Carpenter and his godson Daniel Davies.[2] Contents 1 Critical reception 2 Track listing 3 Personnel 4 References 5 External links Critical reception[edit] Professional ratings Review scores Source Rating AllMusic [3] Upon its release, Lost Themes II received positive reviews from music critics.[4] Track listing[edit] All music is composed by John Carpenter, Cody Carpenter and Daniel Davies.
