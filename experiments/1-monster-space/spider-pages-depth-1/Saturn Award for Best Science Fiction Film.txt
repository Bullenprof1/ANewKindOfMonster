Saturn Award for Best Science Fiction Film Awarded for Best motion picture of the year in the science fiction genre Country United States Presented by Academy of Science Fiction, Fantasy and Horror Films First awarded 1972 Currently held by Ready Player One (2019) Website saturnawards.org The Saturn Award for Best Science Fiction Film is one of the Saturn Awards that has been presented annually since 1972 by Academy of Science Fiction, Fantasy and Horror Films to the best film in the science fiction genre of the previous year.
