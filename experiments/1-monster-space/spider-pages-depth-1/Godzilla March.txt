It premiered in the film King Kong vs. Godzilla, the third film in the franchise, and is the only theme used more times in the Godzilla films than the first theme song. The Godzilla March was used as the main title track for Mothra vs. Godzilla, the fourth film in the franchise, with an altered ending. Hip Hop artist Pharoahe Monch used excerpts and samples from the Mothra vs. Godzilla version of the Godzilla March for his song "Simon Says". He was sued for using the excerpts and samples without permission from Toho.
