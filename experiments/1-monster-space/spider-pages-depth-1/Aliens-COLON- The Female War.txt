(June 2015) (Learn how and when to remove this template message) The Female War Paperback edition Author Steve Perry, Stephani Perry Country United States Language English Genre Science fiction Publisher Bantam Books Publication date July 1, 1993 Media type Print (Paperback) Pages 304 pp ISBN 978-0553561593 Preceded by Nightmare Asylum  Aliens: The Female War is the title of a sci-fi novel by Steve and Stephani Perry, set in the fictional Alien movie universe and adapted from the comic book story "Earth War". It is the conclusion to a series also including Aliens: Earth Hive and Nightmare Asylum.[1] The book was published by Bantam Books on July 1, 1993.
