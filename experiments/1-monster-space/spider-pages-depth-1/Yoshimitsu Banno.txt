Find sources: "API" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR (February 2018) Yoshimitsu Banno Born (1931-03-30)March 30, 1931 Imabari, Ehime, Japan Died May 7, 2017(2017-05-07) (aged 86) Kawasaki, Japan[1] Nationality Japanese Occupation Director Yoshimitsu Banno (坂野 義光, Ban'no Yoshimitsu, March 30, 1931 – May 7, 2017)[2] was a Japanese film director, best known for the cult-classic Godzilla vs. Hedorah, which he directed and co-wrote. Banno was a special guest at G-Fest XII in 2005. He was an executive producer on Legendary Pictures's American Godzilla films, Godzilla (2014), Godzilla: King of the Monsters (2019), and Godzilla vs. Kong (2021). He died of subarachnoid hemorrhage in 2017. King of the Monsters was dedicated to his memory along with Haruo Nakajima.
