Japanese stunt man and actor Toru Kawai (河合 徹, Kawai Tōru) (died 1996)[1] was a Japanese stunt man and actor best known for playing Godzilla in Terror of Mechagodzilla and in episodes of Zone Fighter, a tokusatsu show.
