Japanese film director Koji Hashimoto (橋本 幸治, Hashimoto Kōji, January 21, 1936 – January 9, 2005) was a Japanese film director and producer. He directed the 1984 films Sayonara Jupiter and The Return of Godzilla. He died of coronary disease at age 69 while mountain climbing.
