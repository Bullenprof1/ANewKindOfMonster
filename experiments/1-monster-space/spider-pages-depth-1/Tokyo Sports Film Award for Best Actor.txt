Tokyo Sports Film Award for Best Actor Awarded for Best Performance by an Actor Country Japan Presented by Tokyo Sports First awarded 1991 Website www.tokyo-sports.co.jp/tospo_movie/ The Tokyo Sports Film Award for Best Actor is an award given at the Tokyo Sports Film Award.[1] This award is not only given to a person, but also a film character such as Godzilla in 1992.
