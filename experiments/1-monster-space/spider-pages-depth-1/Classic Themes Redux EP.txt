Classic Themes Redux EP EP by John Carpenter Released June 17, 2016 (2016-06-17) Genre Electronic film score Length 12:29 Label Sacred Bones John Carpenter chronology Lost Themes II (2016) Classic Themes Redux EP (2016) Anthology: Movie Themes 1974–1998 (2017) Classic Themes Redux EP is an extended play by American film director and composer John Carpenter. It was released on June 17, 2016, through Sacred Bones Records.[1] The extended play was created in collaboration with Carpenter's son Cody Carpenter and his godson Daniel Davies.[1] Contents 1 Track listing 2 Personnel 3 References 4 External links Track listing[edit] All music is composed by John Carpenter.
