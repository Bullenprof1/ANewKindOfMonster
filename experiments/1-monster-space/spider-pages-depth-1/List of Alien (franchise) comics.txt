The creative team consisted of Archie Goodwin as the scriptwriter and Walt Simonson as the artist. It won the Harvey Award for Best Graphic Album of Previously Published Work in 2013. Dark Horse Comics[edit] Main article: Aliens (Dark Horse Comics line) Dark Horse Comics is the most well-known publisher of Aliens comics, having published many limited series from 1988 to 1999.
