Littlefield Country of origin United States Headquarters location Lanham, Maryland Distribution National Book Network (US) NBN International (UK) Imprints Many Official website rowman.com Rowman & Littlefield Publishing Group is an independent publishing house founded in 1949. Under several imprints, the company offers scholarly books for the academic market, as well as trade books. The company also owns the book distributing company National Book Network based in Lanham, Maryland. Contents 1 History 2 Imprints 3 Distribution 4 See also 5 References 6 Further reading 7 External links History[edit] The current company took shape when University Press of America acquired Rowman & Littlefield in 1988 and took the Rowman & Littlefield name for the parent company. Since 2013, there has also been an affiliated company based in London called Rowman & Littlefield International. It is editorially independent and publishes only academic books in Philosophy, Politics & International Relations and Cultural Studies. The company sponsors the Rowman & Littlefield Award in Innovative Teaching, the only national teaching award in political science given in the United States.
