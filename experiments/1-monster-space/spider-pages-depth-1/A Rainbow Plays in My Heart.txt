A Rainbow Plays in My Heart Directed by Ishirō Honda Produced by Toho Distributed by Toho Release date 9 July 1957 (1957-07-09)[1] Country Japan Language Japanese A Rainbow Plays in My Heart (わが胸に虹は消えず, Waga mune ni niji wa kiezu) is a 1957 black and white Japanese film directed by Ishirō Honda.
