(1996) Professional ratings Review scores Source Rating AllMusic [1] Village of the Damned is a soundtrack by John Carpenter and Dave Davies for the 1995 film of the same name. It was released in 1995 through Varèse Sarabande. On October 2, 2020...Varese Sarabande Records released a deluxe edition of the score on cd...with the film score, original album and bonus tracks.
