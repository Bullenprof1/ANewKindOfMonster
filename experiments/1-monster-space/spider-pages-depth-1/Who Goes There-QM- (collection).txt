Campbell Jr. Cover artist Hannes Bok Country United States Language English Genre Science fiction Publisher Shasta Publishers Publication date 1948 Pages 230 OCLC 2576424 Who Goes There? is a collection of science fiction stories by American writer John W. Campbell Jr.. It was published in 1948 by Shasta Publishers in an edition of 3,000 copies, of which 200 were signed by Campbell. The 1951 film The Thing from Another World, and 1982 version The Thing by John Carpenter, are based on the title story. The stories originally appeared in the magazine Astounding SF under Campbell's pseudonym Don A. Stuart. Contents 1 Contents 2 Reception 3 References 4 Sources Contents[edit] "Who Goes There?"
