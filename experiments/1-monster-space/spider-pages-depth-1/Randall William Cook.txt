American special effects artist Randall William Cook Born 1951 (age 69–70) United States Other names Randy Cook Education UCLA School of Film Occupation Visual effects artist Years active 1975-2005 Randall William Cook (born 1951) is an American special effects artist most known for The Lord of the Rings trilogy. He worked on The Brave Little Toaster, and is known for his visuals on film and television.
