Find sources: "API" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR (July 2019) Adephagia (/ædiˈfeɪdʒiə/,[1] Ancient Greek: Ἀδηφαγία) in Greek mythology was the goddess and personification of gluttony. She is only mentioned in one source, as having a temple on the island of Sicily, at which she was worshipped alongside Demeter.
