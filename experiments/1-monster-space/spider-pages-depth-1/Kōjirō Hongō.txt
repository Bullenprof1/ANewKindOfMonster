Japanese actor Kōjirō Hongō Hongō in Daibosatsu-toge, 1960 Born (1938-02-15)15 February 1938 Okayama, Japan Died 14 February 2013(2013-02-14) (aged 74) Yokohama, Japan Occupation Actor Years active 1959-2001 Kojiro Hongo (本郷功次郎, Hongō Kōjirō, 2 February 1938 – 14 February 2013) was a Japanese actor.[1] Hongo won an Elan d'or Award for Newcomer of the Year in 1959.[2] His notable international performances were in the Daiei Studios Gamera films.
