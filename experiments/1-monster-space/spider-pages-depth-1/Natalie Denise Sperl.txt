Contents 1 Career 2 Filmography 3 References 4 External links Career[edit] An Esquire cover girl and former Coors Light Girl, starring with Kid Rock in the Coors Light Super Bowl commercial, Sperl was a high fashion runway model in Europe working for such top designers as Cynthia Rowley and Alexander McQueen at Central St. Martins in London. From there she landed several magazine covers before moving to Los Angeles to be an actress. She is a singer-songwriter and the front woman of the band Kill My Coquette.
