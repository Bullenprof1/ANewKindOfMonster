subgenre of horror film which depicts the Devil and associated wicked themes Not to be confused with films titled Satanic, see Satanic (disambiguation). Poster for The Seventh Victim (1943) with tag line "Slave to Satan!" A Satanic film is a subgenre of horror film, and at times other film genres, that involves the Devil as a concept or a character.
