Stihi is a fire-breathing storm demon in Albanian mythology and folklore, similar to the kulshedra.[1][2][3] Contents 1 Etymology 2 Appearance 3 See also 4 Sources 4.1 Citations 4.2 Bibliography Etymology[edit] The Albanian term stihí is a variant of stuhí, "storm", related to Greek: στοιχείο, "element, spirit".[2] Appearance[edit] A female demon in south Albanian and Italo-Albanian popular belief.
