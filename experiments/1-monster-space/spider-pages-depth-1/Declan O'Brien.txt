O'Brien is known as the director of three films in the Wrong Turn series (2009–2012). O'Brien is the president of Utopia Pictures & Television.[1] Contents 1 Biography 2 Selected credits 3 References 4 External links Biography[edit] His work with Syfy has also included writing for the film The Snake King, and he wrote the screenplay for the The Harpy, which began pre-production on June 26, 2006.[2] O'Brien directed the third, the fourth, and the fifth films of the Wrong Turn series, and wrote the latter two films.
