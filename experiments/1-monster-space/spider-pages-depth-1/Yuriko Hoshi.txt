Japanese actress Yuriko Hoshi 星 由里子 Born Yuriko Shimizu (清水 由里子) (1943-12-06)6 December 1943 Tokyo, Japan Died 16 May 2018(2018-05-16) (aged 74) Kyoto, Japan Occupation Actress Years active 1959–2018 Yuriko Hoshi (星 由里子, Hoshi Yuriko, 6 December 1943 – 16 May 2018), real name Yuriko Shimizu (清水 由里子), was a Japanese actress. She appeared in more than ninety films beginning in 1959.
