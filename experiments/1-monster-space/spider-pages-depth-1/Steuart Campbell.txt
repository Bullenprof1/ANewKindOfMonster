He then gained a degree in mathematics and science from the Open University (BA, 1983).[citation needed] Campbell is the Secretary/Treasurer of the Edinburgh Secular Society.[citation needed] Writings[edit] The Loch Ness Monster: The Evidence. 1986 The Aquarian Press (Thorsons Publishing Group) Wellingborough: ISBN 0850304512; Revised ed. 1991 Aberdeen University Press (Macmillan Pergamon Publishing Corporation) Aberdeen: ISBN 1573921785; 1996 Birlinn Ltd, Edinburgh: ISBN 1874744610; 1997 (without subtitle); Prometheus Books, Amhurst: ISBN 1573921785; 2002 Birlinn Ltd, Edinburgh 1997: ISBN 1841581984).
