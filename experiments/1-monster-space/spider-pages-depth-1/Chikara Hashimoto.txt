Japanese baseball player Chikara Hashimoto Born: (1933-10-20)October 20, 1933 Died: October 11, 2017(2017-10-11) (aged 83) Teams Mainichi Orions, 1950s Chikara Hashimoto (橋本 力, Hashimoto Chikara, 20 October 1933 – 11 October 2017[1]), also known as Riki Hashimoto (はしもと りき, Hashimoto Riki), was a Japanese professional baseball player and actor. Hashimoto played baseball for Mainichi Orions in the 1950s. He was forced to retire in 1958 following an injury, and then joined Daiei Studios.
