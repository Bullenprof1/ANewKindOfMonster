1999 Czech fantasy film Helluva Good Luck Directed by Zdeněk Troška Starring Miroslav Šimůnek, Michaela Kuklová Release date 1999 (1999) Running time 111 minutes Country Czech Republic Language Czech Helluva Good Luck (Czech: Z pekla štěstí) is a 1999 Czech fantasy film directed by Zdeněk Troška. It is inspired by Jan Drda's story "Brave Honza".[1] A sequel was released in 2001.[2] Contents 1 Plot 2 Cast 3 References 4 External links Plot[edit] The film is about a youngster Jan, who falls in love with Markéta. She serves at Castle. Jan is forced to a journey. He retrieves a napkin, allowing him to be invisible, and defeats a dragon.
