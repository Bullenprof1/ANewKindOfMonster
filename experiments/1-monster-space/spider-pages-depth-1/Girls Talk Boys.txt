"Girls Talk Boys" Single by 5 Seconds of Summer from the album Ghostbusters: Answer the Call (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack) Released 15 July 2016 (2016-07-15) Recorded 2016 Genre Funk rock[1] Length 3:36 Label Capitol Hi or Hey RCA Songwriter(s) Eric Frederic Teddy Geiger John Ryan Ammar Malik Producer(s) Ricky Reed 5 Seconds of Summer singles chronology "Jet Black Heart" (2015) "Girls Talk Boys" (2016) "Want You Back" (2018) Ghostbusters soundtrack singles chronology Saw It Coming (2016) Girls Talk Boys (2016) Music video "Girls Talk Boys" on YouTube "Girls Talk Boys" is a song by Australian pop rock band 5 Seconds of Summer, taken from the soundtrack of 2016 American supernatural comedy film Ghostbusters: Answer the Call. The song was written by John Ryan, Teddy Geiger, Ammar Malik, along with Ricky Reed, who also handled the song's production.[2][3] "Girls Talk Boys" was released on 15 July 2016 and has charted in multiple countries, reaching a peak of 21 in Australia, 6 in Scotland, and 28 in the United Kingdom.[4] Contents 1 Composition 2 Music video 3 Track listing 4 Personnel 5 Charts 6 Release history 7 References 8 External links Composition[edit] The song is written in the key of E minor with a common time tempo of 120 beats per minute. The vocals span from D4 to D6 in the song.[5] Music video[edit] The official music video was released on 22 July 2016 and featured scenes from the Ghostbusters film. The video featured the band members in makeshift versions of costumes from the film. A spare car and props that resemble elements from the film were also used in the music video.[6][7] As of August 2018, the music video has over 16 million views.
