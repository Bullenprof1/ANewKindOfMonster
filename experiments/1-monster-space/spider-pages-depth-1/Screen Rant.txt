Screen Rant was launched by Vic Holtreman in 2003,[1] and originally had its primary office in Ogden, Utah.[2][3] Screen Rant has expanded its coverage with red-carpet events in Los Angeles, New York film festivals and San Diego Comic-Con panels.[4][5] The associated YouTube channel has over 7.9 million subscribers and over 3,800 videos.[citation needed] The channel also posts Pitch Meeting videos by Ryan George that each have at least several hundred thousand views.[6] In 2015, Screen Rant was acquired by Valnet Inc., an online media publisher based in Montreal.[7][8] The Screen Rant YouTube channel was created on August 18th, 2008.[9] Although Screen Rant is based in Saint-Laurent, Quebec, the Screen Rant YouTube channel is based in Ogden, Utah.
