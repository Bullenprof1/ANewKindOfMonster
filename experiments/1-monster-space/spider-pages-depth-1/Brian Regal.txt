Brian Regal is an American historian of science, skeptic and writer. He is associate professor of the history of science at Kean University in New Jersey.[1][2] Regal is the author of Searching for Sasquatch: Crackpots, Eggheads and Cryptozoology a scholarly study on cryptozoology.[3][4][5] He has also written on the history of the Jersey Devil.[6][7] Publications[edit] Books Henry Fairfield Osborn, Race and the Search for the Origins of Man (London: Ashgate Press, 2002) Human Evolution: A Guide to the Debates (ABC-CLIO, 2004) Pseudoscience: A Critical Encyclopedia (ABC-CLIO/Greenwood, 2009) Icons of Evolution (Greenwood Press, 2008) Searching for Sasquatch: Crackpots, Eggheads and Cryptozoology (Palgrave, 2011) The Secret History of the Jersey Devil: How Quakers, Hucksters, and Benjamin Franklin Created a Monster (Johns Hopkins University Press, 2018)[8] Papers Richard Owen and the Sea-Serpent.
