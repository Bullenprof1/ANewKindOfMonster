The name is related to the word suffix '-algia' denoting a painful condition.[3] The three known Algea are Lupe (Λύπη – "pain"), Achos (Ἄχος – "grief"), and Ania (Ἀνία – "sorrow").[4] References[edit] ^ Hesiod, Theogony 227 ^ Grimal, Pierre; A.
