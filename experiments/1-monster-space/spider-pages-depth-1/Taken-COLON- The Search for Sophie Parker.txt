The film borrowed the themes and similarities in the Taken franchise, particularly Taken and Taken 2. It premiered on September 21, 2013, on Lifetime.[1][2] Contents 1 Premise 2 Cast 3 References 4 External links Premise[edit] After her daughter is abducted in Russia, an NYPD Detective goes out to find and save her from the kidnappers.
