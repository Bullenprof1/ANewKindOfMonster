Encyclopedia Cthulhiana is a supplement published by Chaosium in 1994 for the horror role-playing game Call of Cthulhu. Contents 1 Description 2 Reception 3 Awards 4 Reviews 5 References Description[edit] Encyclopedia Cthulhiana is a 400-page book by Daniel Harms that contains an alphabetized listing of entities, cults and lore from H.P. Lovecraft's Cthulhu Mythos that were published during the twentieth century. Sources include works by Ramsey Campbell, Robert Bloch, and Stephen King, as well as scenarios from the Call of Cthulhu game. There are also suggestions about how this information could be included in a Call of Cthulhu adventure or campaign. An expanded 423-page second edition was published in 1998.
