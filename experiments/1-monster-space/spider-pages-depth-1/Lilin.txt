For other uses, see API (disambiguation). In ancient Mesopotamian religion, lilin were hostile night spirits that attacked men. In Jewish mythology, Lilin (Hebrew: לילין) is a term for night spirits. In Targum Sheni Esther 1:3 King Solomon had lilin dance before him.[1][2][3][4] In the Syriac Apocalypse of Baruch, lilin come from the desert[5] and they are similar to shedim.[6] In popular culture[edit] In the anime series Neon Genesis Evangelion, Lilin is another name for the human race, derived from the angel Lilith. In Highschool DxD, Lilin is the alias of Rizevim Livian Lucifer, the son of the Devil King Lucifer and Lilith, the biblical mother of devils.
