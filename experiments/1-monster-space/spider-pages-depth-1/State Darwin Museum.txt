State Darwin Museum, Moscow, 57/1 Vavilova St. The State Darwin Museum (Russian: Государственный Дарвиновский музей) is a natural history museum in Moscow. The museum was founded in 1907 by Alexander Kohts (1880–1964) and was the world's first museum of evolution[1] explaining the work of Charles Darwin as a causal explanation of nature.
