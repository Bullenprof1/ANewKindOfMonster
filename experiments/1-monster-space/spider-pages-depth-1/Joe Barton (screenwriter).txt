British screenwriter Joe Barton Born 1985 Nationality British Occupation Screenwriter Known for Giri/Haji, Humans Joe Barton is a British screenwriter of film and television. Contents 1 Early life 2 Career 3 Filmography 3.1 Television 3.2 Film 4 References 5 External links Early life[edit] Barton was born in London. He studied Film and Television Production at the University of Westminster, and one of his first jobs was as a driver on Cass. Career[edit] His writing career began in film. He has written films such as The Ritual,[1] My Days of Mercy[2] and IBoy. In 2020, it was announced he had written Invasion, a sci-fi thriller starring Riz Ahmed and Octavia Spencer.[3] Amazon Studios will distribute.[4] He is tapped to write a sequel to Cloverfield.[5] Barton has also written television, first writing for Beaver Falls.[6] He later wrote for Humans, Our World War and the epic miniseries Troy: Fall of a City. In 2019, BBC Two broadcast Barton's crime thriller Giri/Haji (Japanese: 義理/恥, "Duty/Shame"). The series is set in London and Tokyo, with dialogue in English and Japanese.[7][8] On December 13, 2020, it was announced Barton would be showrunner on Netflix's Half Bad, based on the trilogy of books by author Sally Green, with Andy Serkis and Jonathan Cavendish as executive producers.[9] In January 2021, it was announced that Barton would be replacing Terence Winter as showrunner on an untitled Gotham City Police Department spinoff series based on The Batman.[10] Filmography[edit] Television[edit] Production Episodes Broadcaster Beaver Falls Episode #2.5 (2012) E4 Blackout TV Movie (2013) Channel 4 Glasgow Girls TV Movie (2014) BBC Three Our World War Series creator, all episodes (2014) BBC Three Cuffs Episode #1.4 (2015) BBC One Humans Episode #1.4 (2015) Episode #2.4 (2016) Episode #2.6 (2016) Channel 4 Troy: Fall of a City "Battle on the Beach" (2018) BBC One/Netflix Giri/Haji Series creator, all episodes (2019) BBC Two/Netflix Extinction Series creator, all episodes[11] (2022) Sky Half-Bad Series creator (TBA) Netflix Untitled The Batman spin-off Lead writer (TBA) HBO Max Film[edit] Production Director Distributor Nowhere Left to Run (2010) Julian Gibbs Universal Island Records iBoy (2017) Adam Randall Netflix The Ritual (2017) David Bruckner Netflix My Days of Mercy (2017) Tali Shalom Ezer Lionsgate Invasion (TBA) Michael Pearce Amazon Studios Untitled Cloverfield sequel (TBA) J.
