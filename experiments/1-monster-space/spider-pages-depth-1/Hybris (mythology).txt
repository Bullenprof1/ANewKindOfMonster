Hybris[pronunciation?] (Ancient Greek: Ὕβρις means "hubris") was a spirit (daemon) or goddess of insolence, violence, and outrageous behaviour. In Roman mythology, the personification was Petulantia,[1] who reflected the Greek conception of hubris. References[edit] ^ "Hybris". Theoi Greek Mythology. Retrieved 23 February 2016.
