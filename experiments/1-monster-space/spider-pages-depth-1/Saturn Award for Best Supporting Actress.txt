Find sources: "API" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR (March 2015) (Learn how and when to remove this template message) Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress Current recipient: Zendaya Awarded for Best performance of the year by a female in a supporting role in a genre film Country United States Presented by Academy of Science Fiction, Fantasy and Horror Films First awarded 1974/75 Currently held by Zendaya for Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019) Website www.saturnawards.org The following is a list of Saturn Award nominees and winners for Best Supporting Actress, which rewards the best female supporting performance in a genre film. Anne Ramsey and Tilda Swinton are the only actresses to win this award multiple times (twice), while only Whoopi Goldberg and Mercedes Ruehl have won both the Saturn Award and the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for the same role.
