Kimiyoshi Yasuda 安田公義 Born February 15, 1911 Tokyo, Japan Died July 26, 1983 Japan Resting place Japan Notable work Yokai Monsters: Along With Ghosts Kimiyoshi Yasuda (安田公義, Yasuda Kimiyoshi) (born February 15, 1911 Tokyo, Japan,[1] died July 26, 1983) was a Japanese film director from the 1950s to 1970s. He directed at least six films about Zatoichi, the Blind Swordsman.
