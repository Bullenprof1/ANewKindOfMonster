Níðhöggr is identified as a dragon in the Völuspá. Jörmungandr, also known as the Midgard Serpent, is described as a giant, venomous beast. Fáfnir is turned into a dragon as part of the Völsung Cycle. The Gesta Danorum contains a description of a dragon killed by Frotho I. This article relating to a Norse myth or legend is a stub.
