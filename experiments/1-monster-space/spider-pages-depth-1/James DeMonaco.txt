American screenwriter, director, and producer James DeMonaco Born (1969-10-12) October 12, 1969 (age 51) Brooklyn, New York City New York, United States Nationality American Occupation Film director, screenwriter, producer Known for The Purge series James DeMonaco (born October 12, 1969) is an American screenwriter, director, and producer. He is best known for creating the Purge franchise, writing all four films in the series and directing the first three, The Purge (2013),[1] The Purge: Anarchy (2014), and The Purge: Election Year (2016).[2] DeMonaco's first produced screenplay was the Robin Williams comedy Jack (1996), directed by Academy Award-winner Francis Ford Coppola.
