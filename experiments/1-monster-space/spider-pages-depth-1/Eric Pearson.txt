American screenwriter Eric Pearson Occupation Screenwriter Years active 2010–present Known for Marvel One-Shots Eric Pearson is an American screenwriter. He has worked with Marvel Studios, writing various short films for their Marvel One-Shots series of films,[1] and writing the screenplay for the feature films Thor: Ragnarok (2017), Black Widow (2021), and Godzilla vs. Kong (2021).[2][3][4] Contents 1 Early life and career 2 Filmography 2.1 Films 2.2 Television 3 References 4 External links Early life and career[edit] He studied screenwriting at New York University's Tisch School of the Arts.
