API Born Scott Weston Terra (1987-06-25) June 25, 1987 (age 33) Connecticut, U.S. Occupation Actor Years active 1997—2010 Scott Weston Terra (born June 25, 1987) is a former American actor perhaps best known for his role as Mike Parker of the 2002 film Eight Legged Freaks and Young Matthew Murdock in the 2003 film Daredevil. Terra was officially announced as a part of the cast in March 2002.[1] Terra also played Sam Finney in Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star.
